What are Carnivores?

Carnivores are creatures that primarily consume other creatures as a source of food. They are very humble creatures that prefer the delicacy that is other living things. By no means should you fear these rather timid and sizeable creatures as they are docile when startled. If you ever confront a living, breathing specimen, please remain calm and proceed to the nearest safe zone.
