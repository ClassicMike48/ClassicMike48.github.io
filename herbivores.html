What are Herbivores?

Herbivores are the ancestors to vegetarians. They prefer to eat leafy plants that will provide them strength and nobility like the great Pop-eye the Sailor! Characteristics of herbivores include, but are not limited to: large spikes, tails, horns, and other unfriendly things with a point. As the saying goes, it is not easy being green, so do not tempt these simple yet dangerous creatures by disguising yourself as their most attractive snack!

Fun Facts!

  • Green is their favorite food, and color. Its all really the same thing
  • 'Beefy Boys' is not a proper nickname for these hostile giants. They prefer 'Large Legumes'
  • Herbivores always eat their veggies first, since they don't care for the rest anyway.
